### About Me
I am a **3D Artist** and **Level Designer** based in Italy, specializing in environmental design, advanced asset creation, and technical optimization for GTA V and FiveM. As a core member of the **3DR Mapping** team, I focus on the visual experience while maintaining rigorous technical standards.

My workflow is built around **smart modeling practices**: producing clean topology, eliminating redundant geometry, and ensuring all assets are fully optimized for real-time rendering. I bridge the gap between high-end visual quality and game-engine performance.

### Work & Commissions
* 🌐 **Team:** Core 3D Artist at **3DR Mapping**
* 💼 **Commissions:** Available for custom requests, high-performance environments, and professional freelance work.

### Technical Specialization
* **Smart 3D Modeling & Topology:** Clean, production-ready wireframes. Efficient polygon distribution, eliminating unnecessary geometry/n-gons while preserving high-fidelity silhouettes.
* **Engine Optimization:** Strict drawcall management, texture budget control (atlasing), and LOD (Level of Detail) creation to prevent texture loss and frame drops on FiveM servers.
* **Texturing & PBR Workflows:** High-quality PBR material creation (Albedo, Normal, Roughness, Specular) optimized specifically for the GTA V shading system.
* **Level Design & World Building:** Immersive map layouts, precise prop placement, and realistic environmental storytelling.
* **Lighting & Atmosphere:** Custom light placement, emission setups, and ambient color grading to ensure seamless day/night transitions.
* **Structural Layouts & MLO Design:** Proper portal setup, room definition, and clean collisions (ycol) for flawless player interaction.
